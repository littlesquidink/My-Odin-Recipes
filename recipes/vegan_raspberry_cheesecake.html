Vegan Raspberry Cheesecake

My boyfriend LOVES cheesecake!
So, I created this recipe as a healthier, vegan version of his favorite treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 graham cracker crust (premade)
  • 16 ozs of vegan cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 8 ozs of fresh raspberries
  • vanilla extract
  • agar agar powder

Steps: For the filling

  1. In a bowl combine the cream cheese, vanilla extract, and 1 cup of sugar
  2. Use a hand mixer and blend until the sugar is well incorporated
  3. Scoop all the cream cheese mix into the graham cracker crust
  4. Place this into the fridge to set

Steps: For the topping

  1. Add half the container of raspberries and 1/2 cup of sugar to a pot
  2. Heat this on medium/low heat and stir continuously
  3. Let simmer for about 5 minutes
  4. Let cool for 30 minutes
  5. Place sugared raspberries into a blender and blend until smooth
  6. Pass this through a sieve to get rid of the seeds

Steps: For assmembly

  1. Remove the cheesecake from the fridge
  2. Pour the raspberry mixture over the cheesecake
  3. Add fresh raspberries to the top in a pretty design (However you want!)
  4. Cut a slice and enjoy!
