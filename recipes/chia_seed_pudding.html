Chia Seed Pudding

I created this recipe for my boyfriend who eats a lot of sugary yogurt.
I wanted him to have a healthier option with ltos of good vitamins!

Ingredients

  • 5 tablespoons of chia seeds
  • 1 can of coconut milk (8oz)
  • 2 teaspoons of agave
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • Sliced strawberries (for topping)
  • blueberries (for topping)

Steps

  1. Dump the whole can of coconut milk into a pot with the agave, vanilla, and ground cinnamon
  2. Stir continuously on medium heat until the mixture starts to simmer
  3. Remove from the heat and add the chia seeds
  4. Stir very well to make sure all the chia seeds have been saturated
  5. Let cool in the pot for about 30 minutes
  6. Pour out into a glass container
  7. Store in the refigerator until cool
  8. Dish out 1 cup of the chia seed pudding into a small cup or bowl
  9. Add the sliced strawberries and blueberries to the top
  10. Enjoy your guilt free, healthy snack!
